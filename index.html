5 Food Delivery Service Apps to Help You Eat Delicious Meals

We present to your attention one of the top applications for food delivery.

1. goPuff

Shipping cost: Fixed rate of $1.95.

Get snacks, drinks and ice cream, as well as thousands of other products delivered right to your doorstep.

2. Delivery.com

Shipping cost: depends on the restaurant.

Find a local favorite and discover new ones with this app and website. And if there's one thing that sets Delivery.com apart from the rest, it's that they deliver food, alcohol, groceries, gifts, and laundry. Yes, laundry. Best of all, you can earn shipping points with each purchase and redeem them for rewards.


3. Instacart

Shipping Fee: $5.99 per shipping or $0 (with an annual membership of $149).

Get thousands of groceries and other essentials from stores where you're already shopping, with same-day delivery delivered straight to your door.

4. Munchery

Shipping Cost: $8.95 membership.

Chilled meals from the chef will be delivered directly to your door on schedule. Best of all, the prices for these dishes are much lower than for similar dishes in restaurants. And with every food purchase, Munchery donates food to a person in need. This service is a great combination of a food delivery service and a food delivery app.

5. Eat 24 

Shipping cost: depends on the restaurant.

Get easy access to thousands of local restaurants and favorites with delivery straight to your door. Best of all, they now work on GrubHub.
